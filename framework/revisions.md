---

## Versioning policy

LDWA uses a `MAJOR.MINOR` version scheme.

- **MAJOR** bumps for structural changes — adding or removing a pillar, restructuring the review process, or any change that invalidates a prior review's scoring.
- **MINOR** bumps for content additions — new best practices, new lenses, new labs, clarifications to existing material.
- **Patch-level changes** (typos, link fixes, minor wording) are not versioned.

Each pillar and lens carries its own version in its index page. The framework version is the version of the framework documents themselves (introduction, design principles, definitions, review process).

## Revision cadence

The full framework is reviewed annually. Pillars and lenses may be revised more frequently when LaunchDarkly ships a product change that materially affects their content. Major LaunchDarkly product GAs are the typical trigger.

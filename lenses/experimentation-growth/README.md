# Lens: Experimentation / Growth

> *When experimentation is the workload, not the side dish.*

**Status: Phase 3 — draft scheduled, not yet written.**

This lens specializes LDWA for teams whose primary use of LaunchDarkly is experimentation — growth teams, product-led teams, conversion optimization teams. It is the lens that goes deep on experiment design, holdout strategy, organizational decision-making, and the disciplined treatment of experiments as the team's product.

## When this lens will apply

- Your team runs many concurrent experiments and the experiment volume itself is a workload.
- Your business model depends on continual product optimization (subscription growth, conversion, engagement).
- You manage holdouts, primary/secondary metrics, and decision cycles as core product activities.

## Phase 3 scope

The lens will cover experiment portfolio management, holdout strategy at scale, the relationship between concurrent experiments and statistical power, the organizational decision-review cadence, the integration with the team's analytics stack, and the cultural practices that make a high-experiment-volume team work.

In the meantime, the [Experimentation pillar](../../pillars/experimentation/) (Phase 2) will be the foundation; this lens will sit on top.
